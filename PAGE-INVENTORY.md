## Difficulty Criteria

| Level | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **Easy** | Single form or simple display, minimal state, 1–2 components |
| **Medium** | Multi-section layout, search/filter, 3–5 components, service layer required |
| **Hard** | Multi-step flow or complex data relationships, 6+ components, routing between views |
